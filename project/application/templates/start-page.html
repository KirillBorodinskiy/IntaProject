Battleship is a classic two-player strategy game where opponents attempt to sink each other's fleet of ships.

Each player has their own grid where they place their ships, while also having an identical grid used for tracking opponent's fleet

Each player’s fleet consists of several ships of varying lengths, such as an aircraft carrier, battleship, submarine, and destroyer.

The game is played in turns, with each player choose specific coordinates on the grid in an effort to locate and sink their opponent’s ships.

If the guessed coordinate hits part of a ship, it's marked as a "hit", and if it misses, it's marked as a "miss".

The first player to sink all of the opponent's ships wins the game.

To make you new board choose "Create a new board" on the navigation menu
If you already have your board click "Choose an opponent"
If you are willing to see games you participated in, click "My games"
